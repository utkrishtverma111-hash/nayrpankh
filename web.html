Who We Are

Giving Wings to
Forgotten Dreams

Founded in 2020, NayePankh (Hindi for "New Wings") is a grassroots nonprofit dedicated to breaking the cycle of poverty through holistic youth empowerment — one child at a time.

  • Free quality education and digital literacy programs for children aged 6–18
  • Career mentorship pairing students with working professionals
  • Nutrition, mental health support, and safe learning environments
  • Community training for parents and local leaders
  • Scholarship placements in partner schools and colleges

What We Do

Programs Built to
Last a Lifetime

Each program is designed with the child's entire journey in mind — not just a moment.

📖

Udaan — Education First

Structured after-school tutoring, digital literacy labs, and critical thinking workshops for children in government schools.

Ages 6–14
💻

DigiPankh — Tech Skills

Coding bootcamps, AI literacy, and internet safety programs that prepare tomorrow's workforce in rural and semi-urban centres.

Ages 14–18
🎨

Rang — Arts & Expression

Music, visual arts, and theatre as pathways to confidence, emotional resilience, and creative thinking.

All ages
🏥

Sehat — Health & Well-being

Monthly health check-ups, nutritional support, and child-friendly mental wellness sessions with trained counsellors.

All ages
🌍

Udyam — Career Pathways

One-on-one mentorship, internship placements, and enterprise skills for young adults ready to enter the workforce.

Ages 17–24
🏘️

Parivar — Family Support

Financial literacy, parenting workshops, and legal awareness programs for families so the community rises together.

Families
